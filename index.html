Biography

Lauren Nychelle- Lighting Designer.

Lauren Nychelle is a 2nd year MFA candidate at NYU Tisch Department of Design and Film.

Her first short film premiered at Provincetown International Film Festival this year, won Best Comedy Short and will have a NY premiere next week. She filmed her first music video and wrapped her second short film this summer.

Past works included: Ladies' Lady: A New Musical, Picnic, and several pieces with Second Avenue Dance Company.
